Our Main Objective:

Lakas ng Thrift is a retail thrift store that promotes recycling and re-use of used clothing, accessories, and other household goods it is our little space to share our preloved items for a more sustainable life. This is our way of driving change in a world that is facing the daunting task of tackling both climate change and unprecedented biodiversity loss.

Everything is Below 100Php?

Truly to help others acquire things at a price that is affordable & offer a way to thrift shop in the midst of a pandemic. Most other websites that market themselves as "Thrift Stores" charge an upscale price for their used clothing & goods which makes them just like any other place. We sell things for the price at which they just belong, below hundred pesos!
